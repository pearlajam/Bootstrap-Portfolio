About Me

I'm from Central Texas and currently studying at the University of Utah's Coding Boot Camp. I came to Utah to try something different and master skills I had always been interested in. My major background in coding goes way back to the days of Myspace. Even though it seems so long ago, it stayed with me and I never believed my interest would move me here to Utah. I am first generation and greatly value my family and the hardships my parents have endured to get me where I need to be. I am a hard worker who has experience in a number of industries.

  • Trying new food
  • Spending time with family and friends
  • Collecting records
  • Photography
  • Taking long drives and listening to music

My current career goals are, in order of priorities:

  1. Strengthen my skills in front-end coding
  2. Complete the web developer's boot camp
  3. Apply my new skills to a future career
